## Company overview — 150-word version

BOATship is a design and PR studio based in Jimbocho, Tokyo, founded in 2022. We call ourselves a "Business Art Team": a small crew working where design, storytelling, and business strategy overlap.

We focus on three things. First, brand development for the Japanese market — adapting identity, tone, and visual language so brands feel trustworthy to Japanese audiences. Second, company media production — we design, write, and run owned media end to end, with a GA4-based measurement loop that keeps content improving. Third, PR strategy — planned with the PESO model as a repeatable rhythm of touchpoints, not a one-off launch.

One thing makes us unusual: our equal exchange compensation model. Alongside standard fees, we accept payment in other forms of value — a farm pays for its website in vegetables; a co-working brand paid in workspace. It keeps us close to the people we work with. (We accept yen, too.)

## Differentiators（得意領域・差別化要素）

- **Equal exchange compensation** — we accept payment in value other than money (produce, workspace, skills). No other Japanese agency leads with this; it is the fastest way for overseas buyers to remember us. It also signals how we work: flat, honest, relationship-first.
- **Cross-cultural voice** — we reconstruct brand stories for Japanese audiences rather than translate them; English-speaking point of contact (founder).
- **Evidence-based owned media** — our own media publishes research-backed articles and tunes them on measured read rates; we build the same loop for clients.
